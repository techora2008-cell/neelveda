Pure Potency

Sourced from the Natural Botanical Ingredients

Discover the transformative power of the rare botanicals that make Neelveda a masterpiece of Ayurvedic wisdom.

Virgin Coconut Oil

Cold-pressed to preserve its vital nutrients, our coconut oil penetrates deep into the hair shaft to provide weightless moisture and protect against environmental damage.

Bhringraj

Known as the 'King of Hair' in Ayurveda, Bhringraj is a potent herb that promotes hair growth, prevents premature greying, and reduces hair fall by revitalizing the scalp at a cellular level.

Hibiscus

Rich in Vitamin C and essential amino acids, Hibiscus stimulates hair growth from dormant follicles, conditions the strands deeply, and prevents dryness, leaving your hair incredibly soft.

Gooseberry (Amla)

A powerhouse of antioxidants and Vitamin C, Amla strengthens the hair roots, maintains the natural pigment of your hair, and acts as a natural conditioner to boost shine and volume.

Aloe Vera

Deeply soothing and hydrating, Aloe Vera repairs dead skin cells on the scalp. It acts as a great conditioner and leaves your hair all smooth and shiny while promoting healthy hair growth.
