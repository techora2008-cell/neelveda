Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about your Neelveda experience.

How often should I use Neelveda hair oil?

For best results, we recommend using Neelveda 2-3 times a week. Apply to the scalp and hair, leave it on for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for deep conditioning), and wash with a mild shampoo.

Is Neelveda suitable for all hair types?

Yes, our Ayurvedic formula is designed to be beneficial for all hair types, including color-treated and chemically processed hair. It focuses on scalp health and hair strength at the root.

Are the ingredients 100% natural?

Absolutely. Neelveda is committed to purity. We use only cold-pressed oils and high-quality botanical extracts. Our product is free from mineral oils, parabens, silicones, and synthetic fragrances.

How soon can I see results?

While some notice immediate softness and shine, significant improvements in hair strength and texture usually become apparent after 4-6 weeks of consistent use, as this aligns with the natural hair growth cycle.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship only within India. However, we are working on bringing Neelveda to our international community soon. Stay tuned to our social media for updates!
