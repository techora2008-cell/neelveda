The Self-Care Ritual

How to Use the Neelveda Hair Ritual

Maximize results by incorporating traditional Ayurvedic application methods.

01

Apply to Scalp

Pour a small amount of Neelveda hair oil into your palms. Part your hair into sections and apply the oil directly onto your scalp thoroughly.

02

Massage Gently

Use your fingertips to massage the scalp in gentle circular motions for 5 to 10 minutes. This increases scalp blood flow and aids oil absorption.

03

Leave Overnight

For deep root nourishment and conditioning, leave the oil on for at least 30 to 45 minutes, or overnight for a cinematic hair texture transformation.

04

Wash Mindfully

Rinse with a mild, chemical-free herbal shampoo. Avoid hot water, which strips natural lipids. Repeat 2 to 3 times a week for maximum hair strength. Read our story and tradition to see how each bottle is prepared.
